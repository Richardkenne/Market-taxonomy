Select a niche

Hard FM

Structural and mechanical services: HVAC, electrical, plumbing, lifts, fire safety systems.

Soft FM

Non-structural services: cleaning, security, catering, waste management, landscaping.

Integrated FM

Single-contract FM covering both hard and soft services; complex vendor orchestration.

Smart Building FM

IoT-enabled building management: energy monitoring, predictive maintenance, occupancy data.

How to Choose a Niche

Smart Building FM is the fastest-growing segment — IoT and energy compliance are driving demand. Hard FM has the highest contractor spend and the most fragmented tooling.
